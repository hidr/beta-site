About us

We're a friendly bunch of estate agents, property experts, and tech wizards with a common goal of making life much easier for high street agents.

Our Story

Hystreet started in the same fashion as many tech companies - simply as the idea in the mind of it's founders. However, there was one key difference - unlike other tech companies that set out to solve non-existant problems in industries it's founders have no experience in, we were founded by estate agents, and the experience of estate agents is still what our product is centered around - whether that's when agents are using it, or when they're away from a screen.

You can probably tell from some of the beards and haircuts below, that we're not just estate agents any more. In our journey we've attracted some of the best tech experts, marketing gurus and customer service warriors in the world to us in order to fulfill our goal of not only giving estate agents a cutting-edge product, but industry-leading customer service and a support team that is eager to help you every step of the way.
