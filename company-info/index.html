Company Info

Hystreet is a trading name of HIDR Media Ltd, full information can be found on our terms and conditions page

HIDR Media Ltd is a company founded by estate agents who were tired of online-first, investor-backed estate agency startups taking market share from estate agents who have built up their businesses from scratch by working hard, caring about their customers, and simply being good at what they do. Hystreet is a tool that allows these traditional agents to gain a footing into that share of the market, attract new business, and offer existing customers the convenience of a 24/7 online platform whithout sacrificing on service.
