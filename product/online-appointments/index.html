Book appointments online

Hystreet allows customers to book appointments online at any time of day or night, including valuations and viewings. Other ‘online appointment booking’ services that are effectively no more efficient than a phone call, requiring multiple requests and confirmations of when and where the appointment is taking place, and who is attending. Unlike these other services, Hystreet employs a unique ‘one-click’ confirmation technology that allows all the above to be confirmed with a single click from whoever is booking the appointment. As Hystreet uses active diary management, the platform will automatically check when all required parties are available for the appointment that needs to be carried out, so whether or not it’s just checking against one person’s diary for an initial valuation, or if multiple people and agents need to be present for an accompanied viewing, Hystreet is capable of managing it, booking it in everyone’s diaries, and notifying everyone it needs to – instantly.
